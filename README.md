# excel-dashboard

This project presents an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance across different dimensions such as time, region, country, and product category. The dashboard enables users to explore key business metrics through interactive filters and visualizations, supporting data-driven decision-making.

Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze monthly sales and profit trends.
- Compare sales across different regions and countries.
- Identify high-performing product categories.
- Enable interactive filtering for deeper analysis

  DATASETS

- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity

- Country

  Key Insights

- Total Sales reached 8.02 Million.
- Total Profit generated was 3.73 Million.
- More than 51,000 orders were processed.
- Total quantity sold exceeded 154,000 units.
- Monthly sales remained relatively consistent, with peak sales observed in October.
- The Central region recorded the highest shipping cost.
- Fashion contributed the largest share of product category sales.
- The United States generated the highest sales among all countries.
